get to know me!


Welcome to my corner of the internet! I’m Emelyn, a student in fashion communication, based in Bangalore and Kannur. When I’m not at college, you’ll find me with my laptop editing pictures I’ve taken or working on Procreate – or at least pretending to be productive while doing it. This is a space for me to share my interests in photography, fashion, filmmaking, 3D modelling, and graphic design, fuelled by concerning amounts of caffeine and the occasional existential crisis. This blog is an archive of my (mis)adventures, random thoughts and whatever else pops in my head.
